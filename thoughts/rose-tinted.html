Rose-colored software

On a lazy day you may scroll through nascent projects and each is just drenched with ambition. You look at the screenshots, become more convinced of something… whatever that something is. It's great. It is. Exciting to make a difference. There's always the great work to be done.

So you may try out their next-generation forward-thinking game-changing technology. Tune your senses to the nirvana waiting with pure functional codebases. It even comes with swag! But if you take two steps back – I mean, when I at least take a couple steps back, something dawns on me.

I don't need this at all. None of it.

What are you even doing on the computer?
